FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What is the typical project timeline?

It depends on the project type. A digital menu takes 3-5 days. A complete restaurant website takes 1-2 weeks. AR solutions and 3D stores can take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. We always provide a clear timeline before starting.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We provide post-launch support including bug fixes, content updates, and technical assistance. We also offer monthly maintenance packages for long-term partnership.

What technologies do you use?

We use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Three.js for 3D graphics, WebXR and AR.js for Augmented Reality, Shopify for e-commerce, and various modern tools for design and development. We always choose the best tech for your specific project.

Can I see my project during development?

Absolutely! We believe in transparency. You'll get regular updates, preview links, and milestone demos throughout the development process. Your feedback is incorporated at every stage.

Do I need AR glasses for your AR solutions?

No! Our AR solutions work through regular smartphone cameras using WebAR technology. No app download or special hardware needed. Your customers just open a link and experience AR directly in their browser.

What industries do you serve?

We serve restaurants, retail, fashion, e-commerce, real estate, education, and any business looking to leverage AR and 3D technology. Our solutions are adaptable to any industry.

How do I get started?

Simple! Click the "Start a Project" button below or visit our Contact page. Fill in your details and project requirements. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with a free consultation and project proposal.
